# How to Submit an IT Ticket

A guide for end users on getting help from IT.

---

## 🎫 Why Submit a Ticket?

Submitting a ticket (instead of just calling or stopping by) helps because:

- **Your issue is tracked** — It won't be forgotten
- **You get updates** — See progress and resolution
- **It's documented** — Reference for future issues
- **IT can prioritize** — Urgent issues get handled first

---

## 📝 How to Submit a Ticket

### Option 1: IT Help Portal (Recommended)

1. Go to **https://helpdesk.company.com**
2. Log in with your work email
3. Click **"Submit a Request"** or **"New Ticket"**
4. Fill out the form:
   - **Subject:** Brief description
   - **Description:** Detailed explanation
   - **Category:** Select type of issue
   - **Priority:** How urgent?
5. Click **Submit**
6. You'll receive a confirmation email with ticket number

### Option 2: Email

Send an email to **helpdesk@company.com**

A ticket will be automatically created from your email.

### Option 3: Phone (Urgent Issues)

Call **x1234** (internal) or **(555) 123-4567** (external)

Use this for:
- Can't log in at all
- Complete system outage
- Security incidents

## ✍️ What to Include in Your Ticket

The more detail you provide, the faster IT can help.

### Good Ticket Example

```
Subject: Outlook not syncing on mobile phone

Description:
I got a new iPhone yesterday and added my work email. 
It worked at first, but now it says "Cannot connect to server."

What I've tried:
- Restarting the phone
- Deleting and re-adding the account
- Connecting to Wi-Fi instead of cellular

My laptop Outlook works fine.

Phone model: iPhone 15
iOS version: 17.4
```

### Bad Ticket Example

```
Subject: Help

Description:
email broken
```

**Why it's bad:** IT doesn't know what "broken" means, what device, what error message, or what you've tried.

---

## 📋 Ticket Checklist

Before submitting, include:

```
☐ What is the problem? (specific description)
☐ When did it start?
☐ What device? (laptop, phone, etc.)
☐ What error message? (exact text or screenshot)
☐ What have you tried already?
☐ Can you still work? (helps with priority)
```

---

## 🚨 Priority Guide

| Priority | Use When | Example |
|----------|----------|---------|
| **Critical** | Can't work at all, multiple people affected | Server down, security breach |
| **High** | One person can't work | Can't log in, laptop won't boot |
| **Medium** | Can work but with difficulty | Slow computer, software bug |
| **Low** | Minor issue, not urgent | Question, minor request |

**Don't mark everything as Critical** — it slows down truly urgent issues.

## 📸 How to Take a Screenshot

Screenshots help IT understand your issue.

### Windows
- **Full screen:** Press `Print Screen` key
- **One window:** Press `Alt + Print Screen`
- **Select area:** Press `Windows + Shift + S`

### Mac
- **Full screen:** Press `Cmd + Shift + 3`
- **Select area:** Press `Cmd + Shift + 4`

### Then:
- Paste into your ticket (Ctrl+V)
- Or save as file and attach

## 🔄 After You Submit

### What to Expect

1. **Confirmation email** — You'll receive a ticket number (e.g., INC0012345)
2. **Assignment** — IT will assign someone to your ticket
3. **Updates** — You'll get emails when status changes
4. **Resolution** — IT will notify you when fixed
5. **Closure** — Ticket is closed after you confirm

### How to Check Status

1. Go to **https://helpdesk.company.com**
2. Click **"My Tickets"** or **"My Requests"**
3. Find your ticket number
4. View status and updates

---

## 💬 Responding to IT

If IT asks for more information:

- **Reply to the email** — Keeps everything in the ticket
- **Be specific** — Answer their questions directly
- **Include screenshots** — If they ask to "show" something
- **Respond promptly** — Tickets can't move forward without info

## 🚫 What NOT to Do

| Don't | Why |
|-------|-----|
| ❌ Submit multiple tickets for same issue | Causes confusion, delays response |
| ❌ Mark everything Critical | Slows down truly urgent issues |
| ❌ Walk to IT desk for non-urgent issues | Submit ticket for tracking |
| ❌ Email individual IT staff directly | Use helpdesk email for proper routing |
| ❌ Provide vague descriptions | Makes troubleshooting harder |

## ❓ FAQs

### "When should I call instead of submit a ticket?"

Call for:
- Can't log in (need immediate help)
- Suspicious security issue
- Complete system outage
- VIP/executive issue

Submit ticket for everything else.

### "My issue is urgent but I marked it Medium"

Reply to your ticket and explain the urgency. IT can upgrade priority.

### "I haven't heard back in 2 days"

1. Check your ticket status online
2. Reply to the ticket email asking for update
3. If still no response, call helpdesk

### "The issue came back after IT fixed it"

Reply to the original ticket (don't create new one) explaining the issue returned.
